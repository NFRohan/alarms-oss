System Access

Permissions Required

To wake you up on time, we need a few things from your system. Don't let the sleep wins.

alarm_on

Exact Alarm

Allows the app to trigger alarms precisely at the set time without system-enforced delays.

notifications_active

Notifications

Required to show the alarm screen and play sounds when it's time to get out of bed.

battery_charging_full

Battery Optimization

Exclude the app from battery restrictions so the alarm isn't killed in the background.
